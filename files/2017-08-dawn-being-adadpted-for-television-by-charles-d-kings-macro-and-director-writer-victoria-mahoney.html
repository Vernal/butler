Ava DuVernay, Charles D. King’s Macro, and Victoria Mahoney Adapting “Dawn” for Television

Dawn, the first book in Octavia Butler’s Xenogenesis Series is being developed for television with by Academy Award–nominated director Ava Duvernay, Charles D. King’s Macro, and writer-director Victoria Mahoney attached to the project.

In an exclusive on Deadline, Dominic Patten reports, “In what remarkably is the first time that Science Fiction Hall of Famer's Butler's work has been adapted for TV, Mahoney will pen the series about an African-American woman who works with aliens to resurrect the human race 250 years after a nuclear war. King and DuVernay will executive produce the Dawn series.”
